F.A.Q.

Why is the server adults-only?

Our minimum age requirement isn’t intended to be a jab at minors, nor does it mean that this space is NSFW in nature. However, on other servers, the majority of the player base tends to be teenagers. We wanted a space to play and hang out with other adults, so we could relate to other players more by having similar life experiences and interests.

What does “Meezer” mean?

It’s a nickname for the Siamese cat breed!

What makes MeezerCraft stand out from other servers?

Aside from being an adult server, we have a custom map with modded terrain generation, cats, that sweet “Mineblr”/Mizuno’s aesthetic™, and a community that welcomes women and LGBT folks. We also try to offer a more organic survival experience than a lot of semi-vanilla servers do. This means that you won’t keep your inventory on death and can’t use the /back command, making the sweet sting of Minecraft death as painful as Mojang intended.

Is being on the Discord server a requirement?

Yes. It’s how we keep track of our user information, and how staff can contact you and vise versa. If you leave the Discord server, you’ll be removed from the in-game whitelist as a result.
