Join Us!

Wanna join the fun? To begin our whitelist process, submit this application, join our Discord, and notify staff through a help ticket.

Your app will be ignored if you don't make a help ticket. This is required!

Discord & Social Rules


  • Don't join if you're not 20+!
  • Respect one another and keep things civil.
  • Cursing is fine, but don't use derogatory language, i.e. racial or anti-LGBT slurs.
  • In general, don't be racist, sexist, homophobic, transphobic, etc.
  • No NSFW, i.e. photos or conversations of graphic nudity, violence, sex, and hate symbols.
  • Have your in-game name in your Discord display name, so we know who you are!


In-Game Rules


  • The Discord rules apply as well.
  • No griefing - this is an immediate ban.
  • No client-side mods or other tools that give you an unfair advantage.
  • No AFK methods (pools, jumping, etc.).
  • No redstone contraptions that cause server-side lag.
  • Builds may not contain NSFW imagery or hate symbols.
  • Build at least 10 chunks away from another player.
  • Build at least 20 chunks away from spawn.


It's every player's responsibility to understand and follow our rules. They're also posted in our Discord server in the #info-and-rules channel to easily refer back to. *

Being on this server is a privilege, not a right, and staff reserves the right to permanently remove you from the server at any time for any reason. We don't take the time to issue multiple warnings/"strikes" or suspensions. *

MeezerCraft being an adult server does not make it acceptable to post NSFW content, or use this as an "e-dating" server. *
