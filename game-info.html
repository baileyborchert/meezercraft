Game Info

  • The map is 30k x 30k blocks.
  • Creeper explosions and fire spread are disabled to protect builds, but both still deal player damage.
  • You lose items on death. Gravestones prevent them from despawning or being looted.
  • PVP is enabled.
  • Difficulty is normal.

Plugins

Note: This is not all-inclusive! There are other plugins installed for admin use, but these are the ones that players will directly interact with in-game through commands.

  • The New Economy - Stores balances for the player economy.
  • BetterRTP - Random teleport command.
  • ColorName - Allows you to change the color of your username.
  • EasyWarp - Warp to events and different places around spawn.
  • Harbor - Speeds up the night if 50% of players in the overworld are in bed.
  • Jobs - Join up to three jobs to generate income.
  • LWC - Configure locks on chests, furnaces, and other “use” items to protect them within your base.
  • QuickShop - Buy and sell items in the player economy with shop chests.
  • SetHome - Set a home to teleport to your base at any time.
  • SimpleTpa - Send teleport requests to other players.
  • UltimateTimber - Easy tree-chopping!
